I am a graduate researcher at the Space Mobility & Robotics Laboratory, Seoul National University. My research focuses on trajectory optimization for free-floating space robots subject to nonholonomic constraints, combining learning-based motion planning with physics-informed surrogate modeling.

I am broadly interested in space robotics, autonomous systems, and reinforcement learning for robotic control in extreme environments.
